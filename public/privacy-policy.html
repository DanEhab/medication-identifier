Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Medication Identifier is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User Information: Name, age, sex, health notes (optional fields you fill in)
  • Product Queries: Pharmaceutical product names you search for or images you upload
  • Saved Items: List of pharmaceutical products you choose to save

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: Features used, search queries, timestamps
  • Camera Access: Only when you explicitly use the camera feature to identify pharmaceutical products

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts or other apps
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Provide Product Information: Process your queries to fetch pharmaceutical product details from AI services
  • Enhance User Experience: Remember your user information across sessions
  • Cache Responses: Store product information to reduce API calls and improve speed
  • Generate Reports: Create PDF/Word documents when you request exports

2.2 Data Processing

  • User information is stored locally on your device only using browser localStorage
  • Product queries are sent to our servers and Google Gemini API for processing
  • Images uploaded are processed but NOT permanently stored
  • Search results are cached in our MongoDB database for up to 180 days

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage (Your Device)

  • User information (name, age, sex, health notes)
  • Saved products list
  • Language preference
  • This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you clear app data

3.2 Server Storage (MongoDB Database)

  • Product search results (cached for 180 days, then auto-deleted)
  • Product names and information returned by AI
  • NO user information is stored on our servers

3.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • MongoDB database uses authentication and access controls
  • API keys are stored securely as environment variables
  • No payment or financial information is collected

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Gemini API

  • Purpose: AI-powered pharmaceutical product identification and information retrieval
  • Data Shared: Product names, uploaded images (not stored by Google)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 MyMemory Translation API

  • Purpose: Translate product information to Arabic
  • Data Shared: English text for translation (no user info)
  • Privacy Policy: MyMemory Usage Terms

4.3 MongoDB Atlas

  • Purpose: Database for caching product information
  • Data Stored: Product search results only (no user data)
  • Privacy Policy: MongoDB Privacy Policy

4.4 Vercel (Hosting)

  • Purpose: Web hosting and serverless functions
  • Data Processed: API requests and responses
  • Privacy Policy: Vercel Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

5.1 Right to Access

You can view all locally stored data in the app (user info, saved products)

5.2 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

  • Local Data: Clear app data or uninstall the app
  • Cached Data: Contact us to request deletion of cached product data

5.3 Right to Data Portability

Export your product information reports as PDF or Word documents

5.4 Right to Object

You can choose not to use features that process your data (e.g., don't upload images, don't fill in user info)

5.5 Right to Rectification

Edit or delete user information anytime within the app

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

7. Data Retention
