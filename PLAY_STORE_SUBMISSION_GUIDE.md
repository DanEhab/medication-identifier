## üÜò TROUBLESHOOTING

### Common Issues:

**1. "Package name already exists"**
- Change in `android/app/build.gradle`: `applicationId`
- Update everywhere and rebuild

**2. "Signature verification failed"**
- You used wrong keystore
- Must use SAME keystore for all uploads

**3. "APK rejected - missing permissions explanation"**
- Go to: Policy ‚Üí App content ‚Üí Permissions
- Explain why you need Camera, Storage permissions

**4. "Content rating incomplete"**
- Complete content rating questionnaire fully
- Answer all questions honestly

**5. "Privacy policy unreachable"**
- Test URL manually
- Ensure privacy-policy.html is publicly accessible
